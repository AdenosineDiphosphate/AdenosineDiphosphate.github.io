Welcome to the Quantum Optics Lab

The long-term goal of our research is to develop and advance nanoscale imaging techniques for extremely-high resolution sensing(chemical, thermal, optical and magnetic) in practical complex environments and see the eventual adoption of these techniques by the scientific community in both academic and industrial settings.

Our research focus is in two areas. One is to develop a transformative nanoscale chemical and thermal imaging microscopy based on advanced plasmonic fiber probes and use it to study materials in application-relevant environments (ambient and liquid). The other is to study surface reaction mechnisms on various catalytic and optic material at the microscale and molecular level.
